Bajawa

Bajawa Island is a hidden gem awaiting discovery by adventurous travelers seeking unspoiled natural beauty and rich cultural experiences. Its rugged landscapes, volcanic peaks, and vibrant traditional villages make it a captivating destination for those craving authentic Indonesian encounters.

Location

Bajawa Island is nestled within the Indonesian archipelago, specifically in the East Nusa Tenggara province. It lies within the Lesser Sunda Islands, offering a picturesque setting surrounded by the Flores Sea to the north and the Sawu Sea to the south.
